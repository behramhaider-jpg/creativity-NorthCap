Public evaluation criteria

This version is built to use only publicly available information. It screens on size, exchange, liquidity, growth, valuation multiples, analyst recommendation summaries when public, and management-team quality using public filings and company websites.

Public source map

  • SEDAR+ filings: annual financials, MD&A, and AIF.
  • Company investor relations pages: management bios and presentations.
  • Exchange or market pages: market cap, trading volume, listing status.
  • Public finance pages: consensus recommendation summaries, if visible.
